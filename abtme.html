About Me



Hi! My name is Greg Terteryan! I'm an Armenian American, and if you've seen other parts of my portfolio, you know that I'm quite the computer... enthusiast? My love for computer technology goes way back to when I was a child. It wasn't anything like "I got to see how computers were made and what make them tick," it was more like, "I saw a computer, pondered for a little bit, and then wondered how they worked." While my curiousity for how they worked wasn't a big priority then, I was still fascinated and in love with the fact that they worked. Such an incredible culmination of knowledge from many different people, all coming together to create such a wonderful tool! That and my undying love of video games is what set the stage for where I am now.
